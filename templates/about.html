Project Limb Rescue

About Project Limb Rescue

Cancer patients are at risk of lymphedema, a devastating chronic complication. Our overall aim is to develop a product helping patients to monitor the risk of lymphedema. The product marries wearable devices equipped with photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors and our custom software to detect changes in limb characteristics that are concerning for new-onset, or worsening lymphedema. Based on the achievements of previous work, we have three specific goals.

Firstly, we will build a web tool that allows users to upload raw data and show a wave figure. The second and core goal is to build the prediction model leveraging the machine learning technology to help with identifying waveform changes over longitudinal data collection points. Then we plan to implement the cloud-based solution to facilitate storage of longitudinal data collection and implementation of the above machine learning features. We expect that based on this home-based monitoring product, the risk of lymphedema in cancer patients will be reduced.
